Methodology
Hybrid Keyword + LLM Scoring Pipeline

Phase 1 — Keyword Pre-Filter: 2,820 text extracts scanned against 6-thread keyword architecture. Scoring: strong (×15) + medium (×5) − false-positive context (×10) + title bonus (20), clamped [0,100]. Co-occurrence boosting upgrades medium→strong when contextually validated.

Phase 2 — LLM Deep Read: 351 candidates (keyword score ≥ 10) sent to Claude for contextual analysis. LLM scores 6 threads + 5 additional dimensions (justice, ethics/religious, gendered labor, disability, informal economy) plus greenwash risk (0-100) and actionability (0-100). LLM scores override keywords where they differ.

Expert Council

16-member council: 12 domain experts (climate, water, energy, nuclear, policy, economics, justice, measurement, corporate sustainability, AI systems, infrastructure, toxicology) + 4 ethics/religious voices (environmental ethics, Abrahamic traditions, Eastern/Indigenous traditions, theology & technology).

Source Corpus

Tapestry — The Global AI Principles & Governance Database (Lin, 2026). ~3,044 statements from ~3,460 organizations across all sectors and regions. Text extracts available for ~2,820 statements.
